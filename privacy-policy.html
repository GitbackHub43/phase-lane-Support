Privacy Policy for Phase Lane

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Phase Lane (“the App”) is a mobile game developed and operated by the app developer (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment or billing information

Apple Services

Game Center

The App uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Apple may process Game Center nicknames, scores, and gameplay-related data. This information is handled entirely by Apple.

In-App Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to payment details or billing information.

Data Storage

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device. Deleting the App removes this locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

Phase Lane is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
