Privacy Policy for Phase Lane

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Thryvenex Holdings LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates Phase Lane ("the App"). This policy describes how we handle your information. We are committed to a privacy-first gaming experience and have built the App to function without collecting any personal information.

Zero Data Collection Policy
We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. The App does not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers (IDFA).

1. Information We Do Not Access

We do not have access to, nor do we request:

  • Your Name or Identity
  • Contact Information (Email, Phone, etc.)
  • Location Data
  • Contacts or Calendar
  • Photos or Files

2. Apple Services & Processing

Game Center (Optional, Opt-In Only)

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for global leaderboards. Submitting scores to the leaderboard is entirely optional and requires your explicit consent. The App will prompt you before submitting any scores, and you may change your preference at any time in the in-game Settings menu via the "Leaderboard" toggle.

If you opt in, your Game Center nickname and high scores are processed solely by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store this information on our own servers. Scores already submitted to Game Center cannot be removed by the App.

In-App Purchases (StoreKit)

Transactions are handled securely by Apple's StoreKit service. When you make a purchase, Apple processes your payment information. We do not receive or store your credit card details, billing address, or any other financial information. Apple provides us with an anonymous transaction receipt to unlock your purchased content. You may restore previous purchases at any time via the "Restore Purchases" button in the Settings menu.

3. Local Storage

To enhance your gameplay, the App stores certain anonymous settings directly on your device (using UserDefaults):

  • Your local High Score
  • Selected Character and Theme preferences
  • Sound and Music toggles
  • Leaderboard consent preference

This data stays on your device. You can completely wipe this information at any time by selecting "Reset Local Progress" in the in-game Settings menu. Deleting the App will also remove this local data.

4. Children's Privacy

Phase Lane does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App contains high-speed visuals and flashing lights; parental discretion is advised.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
