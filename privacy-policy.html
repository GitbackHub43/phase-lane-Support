Privacy Policy for Phase Lane

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Phase Lane (“the App”) is designed to be a high-performance, privacy-first gaming experience. We believe that your data belongs to you, which is why we have built the App to function without collecting any personal information.

Zero Data Collection Policy
We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. The App does not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers (IDFA).

1. Information We Do Not Access

We do not have access to, nor do we request:

  • Your Name or Identity
  • Contact Information (Email, Phone, etc.)
  • Location Data
  • Contacts or Calendar
  • Photos or Files

2. Apple Services & Processing

Game Center (Optional)

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for global leaderboards and achievements. Any data shared via Game Center (such as your nickname and high scores) is processed solely by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store this information on our own servers.

In-App Purchases (StoreKit)

Transactions are handled securely by Apple's StoreKit service. When you make a purchase, Apple processes your payment information. We do not receive or store your credit card details, billing address, or any other financial information. Apple provides us with an anonymous transaction receipt to unlock your purchased content.

3. Local Storage

To enhance your gameplay, the App stores certain anonymous settings directly on your device (using UserDefaults):

  • Your local High Score
  • Selected Character and Theme preferences
  • Sound and Music toggles

This data stays on your device. You can completely wipe this information at any time by selecting "Reset Local Progress" in the in-game Settings menu. Deleting the App will also remove this local data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Phase Lane does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.
